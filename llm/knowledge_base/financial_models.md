## Tax Credits and Incentives

### Historic Tax Credit (reference: US model, adapted to context)
- 20% federal tax credit on qualified rehabilitation expenditures
- Must maintain historic character (ICOMOS compliance)
- Investor transfers credit = attracts institutional equity

### Kazakhstan Tax Benefits
- Property tax exemption for listed heritage buildings (up to 100%)
- VAT reduction on restoration materials (from 12% to 0% for certified works)
- Accelerated depreciation: 10 years instead of 40 for renovated heritage

## ROI Calculation Framework

### Net Present Value (NPV)
NPV = Σ (CF_t / (1+r)^t) - Initial Investment
- CF_t = annual net cash flow in year t
- r = discount rate (typically 8–12% for real estate)
- Evaluate over 10-year horizon minimum

### Simple ROI (for quick assessment)
ROI_years = Net_Investment / Annual_Net_Revenue
Net_Investment = Total_Cost - Tax_Credits - Grants
Annual_Net_Revenue = Gross_Revenue × (1 - Vacancy_Rate) - OpEx

## Revenue Benchmarks (Almaty / Central Asia Market 2024)

### Coworking
- Hot desk: $150–300/month
- Private office (4-6 persons): $800–1,500/month
- Event space: $300–1,500/half-day
- Occupancy target: 70–80%

### Hotel
- Budget boutique: $60–100/night
- Mid-range boutique: $100–200/night
- Premium heritage: $200–400/night
- Occupancy target: 60–75%

### Educational / Office
- Long-term institutional lease: $15–35/m²/month
- University partnership: $10–20/m²/month (lower rate, higher stability)

## Sensitivity Analysis Variables
▶ Key drivers to model:
1. Vacancy rate (±5% changes ROI by ~1 year)
2. Construction cost overrun (heritage projects: budget +20–30% contingency)
3. Operating cost (HVAC in renovated buildings: +15% vs new construction)
4. Revenue growth rate (cultural sector: 3–8% annual growth)

## Financing Structure (Typical)
- Equity: 30–40% (founder + angel investors)
- Senior debt: 40–50% (bank loan, 8–12% interest, 10-year term)
- Grant/subsidy: 10–20% (reduces payback period significantly)
- Tax credit equity: 5–10% (if historic credits available)
